DPR welcomes you #BackToSummer!

As public health guidance allows for fewer restrictions on DPR’s programs and facilities, DC residents are invited #BackToSummer to enjoy:

  • Outdoor Pools
  • Spray Parks
  • Indoor Pools
  • Recreation Centers and Fitness Centers
  • Roving Leader-led movie nights and special events for young people in targeted communities
  • Platinum Saturdays for senior citizens
  • FREE Summer Meals
  • Traditional Summer Camps
  • Camp-At-Home
  • Boost Camps

    • While DC is reopening, we must remain vigilant against the spread of COVID-19. DPR follows the standards set forth by DC Health and CDC on COVID-19. For full information on the District’s response to COVID-19, visit http://coronavirus.dc.gov.

    PLATNIUM SATURDAYS:

    Platinum Saturdays for our seasoned citizens begin Saturday, June 26 2021 and run weekly through August 18, 2021. Platinum Saturdays feature COVID-compliant concerts, bingo, games, activities, and more!

    BOOST CAMPS:

    Boost Camps for selected DC public charter school students to stem COVID-19 learning loss and engage in DPR-led recreation activities (limited to students of participating schools).
